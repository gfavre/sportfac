{% trans "About LAGAPEO file format" %}
{% blocktrans %}

We accept files extracted from LAGAPEO. They should be in .xlsx format and have the mandatory columns named like this:

  • "ID LAGAPEO" or "ELEVE_ID_PERS" or "NO": unique ID of the child
  • "Nom" or "ELEVE_NOM" or "NOM": last name of the child
  • "Prénom" or "ELEVE_PRENOM" or "PRENOM": first name of the child
  • "Genre" or "ELEVE_GENRE" or "SEXE": Gender of the child (values must be f/h or F/G)
  • "Date de naissance" or "ELEVE_DATE_NAISS" or "DAT_NAISSANCE": date of birth of the child

Additional non-mandatory columns can be imported:

  • "AVS" or "N_AVS": AVS number
  • "Année" or "ANNEE" or "ELEVE_ANNEE_SCOL" or "Année2": School year
  • "Nationalité": Nationality
  • "Langue maternelle" or "LANGUE MATERNELLE": mother tongue
  • "Etablissement" or "ETABLISSEMENT" or "ETABLISSEMENT_NOM": School name
  • "Blacklist" or "BLACKLIST": Should the child be blacklisted from registrations
